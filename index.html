About Me

Who am i?

"Who are you? Where are you from?" It's a common question I hear that never fails to trip me up.  Though my family, heritage, and culture lies in West Africa, I was born and spent in the first 11 years of my life in the United States. By middle school, I was living in Johannesburg, South Africa, and by high school, I was admitted into a travelling school where we live and learned in a new country every 3 months. Along my journey, I have picked up a substantial amount of passions that include martial arts, music, entreprenuership, volunteering, and STEM.

Passions

What do I love?

Martial Arts

From the moment I received my first Kung Fu white belt, I knew I was destined for the life of a warrior. I have over 12 years of experience in martial arts and combat sports like boxing, Muay Thai, and Kung Fu. I use my love of martial arts to volunteer at the Lionheart Initiative, a non-profits that creates opportunities in martial arts throughout Africa.

Entreprenuership

Using fashion as a catalyst for change and African prints with empowering storytelling qualities, my award-winning brand Jarabi Fitness’ line of unique African print activewear is designed to make women feel comfortable, confident, and accepted to help empower women to workout. Jarabi Fitness has won 1st place in the Innovator Competition, a national business pitch competition given by the University of Iowa.

Programming

I’ve always loved critical thinking, problem solving, and creating new things. One way I combine all those skills in an engaging way is through computer science and programming. I spread my love of programming through volunteering with NGO's like MLC STEM Center and GirlsWhoSTEAM inc.

Music

“One good thing about music, when it hits you feel no pain,”. These are the first lyrics that sing through my mind whenever I’m asked why I love music. Music effortlessly fills me with such joy and love for the world around me. Using my passion of music and programming, I developed an app called Seventh that uses music therapeutically to help teens manage their emotional wellness.

Medicine

Serving others has always been a passion of mine. Medicine is so important to me as it allows me to serve others while using critical-thinking and problem solving. My passion for medicine has inspired me to attend Virtual Shadowing sessions usually reserved to Pre-Med students, and volunteer work at local hospitals.
